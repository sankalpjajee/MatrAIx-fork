## About this survey

The Federal Reserve's Survey of Household Economics and Decisionmaking (SHED) is an annual survey that measures the economic well-being and financial lives of U.S. households — covering income and employment, banking and credit, savings and retirement, housing, and how families would handle an unexpected expense.
