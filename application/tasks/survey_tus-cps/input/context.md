## About this survey

The Tobacco Use Supplement to the Current Population Survey (TUS-CPS) is sponsored by the National Cancer Institute (NCI) and fielded by the U.S. Census Bureau. It is a key source of national and state tobacco-use data, covering cigarette smoking status and history, cigarettes per day, menthol use, quit attempts and cessation methods, e-cigarette and other tobacco-product use, workplace and home smoking/vaping rules, and public opinion about smoking in public venues.
