# Micron Technology (MU) — Recent Earnings Context

Micron Technology (Nasdaq: MU) reported **record fiscal Q3 2026** results on
June 24, 2026. The quarter ended May 28, 2026.

## Key Financials

| Metric | FQ3-26 | FQ2-26 | FQ3-25 (YoY) |
|--------|--------|--------|---------------|
| Revenue | $41.5B | $23.9B | $9.3B |
| Gross margin | 84.9% | 74.9% | 39.0% |
| Operating income | $33.7B | $16.5B | $2.5B |
| Non-GAAP EPS | $25.11 | $12.20 | $1.91 |
| Operating cash flow | $25.4B | $11.9B | $4.6B |
| Free cash flow | $18.3B | — | — |

- Revenue grew **74% sequentially** and **346% year-over-year**.
- Data center revenue exceeded **$25 billion** in the quarter (annualized
  run-rate >$100B). Data center SSD revenue more than doubled sequentially.
- DRAM revenue: $31.3B (76% of total); NAND revenue: $9.9B (24%).

## Guidance (FQ4 2026)

- Revenue: **$50B ± $1B** (would be another sequential record).
- Non-GAAP EPS: **$31.00 ± $1.00**.
- Gross margin: ~85%.
