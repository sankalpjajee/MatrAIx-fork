## About this survey

The December Food Security Supplement to the Current Population Survey is conducted by the U.S. Census Bureau for the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Economic Research Service (USDA/ERS). It measures the full range of severity of food insecurity in U.S. households — the standard ~18-item household food-security scale (worrying food would run out, being unable to afford balanced meals, cutting or skipping meals, going hungry) plus weekly and usual food spending, minimum-spending need, food-program participation (SNAP, school meals, WIC), and community food assistance.
