## About this survey

The Survey of Public Participation in the Arts (SPPA) is the nation's largest, most representative study of U.S. adults' engagement with the arts. It is sponsored by the National Endowment for the Arts (NEA) and fielded as a supplement to the Current Population Survey by the U.S. Census Bureau. It measures attendance at live arts events, arts consumption through electronic media, personal art-making and performance, arts learning over the lifetime, and related leisure activities.
