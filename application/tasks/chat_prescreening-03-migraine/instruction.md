# Trial pre-screening chat - Digital Behavioral Program for Episodic Migraine Prevention (MIG-CARE)

Chat with the clinical-trial pre-screening assistant as yourself - the assigned persona - about joining the study "Digital Behavioral Program for Episodic Migraine Prevention (MIG-CARE)".

You are considering signing up and want to know whether you qualify. Let the assistant lead the screening: answer its questions honestly from what you know about yourself, and say so when you do not know a value. Do not volunteer your whole history at once unless that is how you naturally talk.

Continue until the assistant gives you its final eligibility assessment. This is a preliminary screening conversation, not medical advice - do not invent clinical details you would not know.
