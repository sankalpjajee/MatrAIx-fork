# Clinical-trial pre-screening assistant

A chatbot that walks a potential study participant through a clinical trial's
inclusion and exclusion criteria to give a preliminary eligibility read. This
task screens for the synthetic study "Digital Behavioral Program for Episodic Migraine Prevention (MIG-CARE)" (episodic migraine prevention).

It must ask about every applicable criterion before concluding, state clearly
that pre-screening is preliminary and the study team makes the final call, and
end with a single structured verdict: likely eligible, likely ineligible, or
insufficient information.

What to expect: systematic criterion-by-criterion questioning, careful handling
of "I don't know" answers, and a disclaimer before the final assessment. The
trial is synthetic (ClinicalTrials.gov style) - no real studies or patients.
