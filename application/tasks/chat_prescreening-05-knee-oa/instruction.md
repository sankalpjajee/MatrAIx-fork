# Trial pre-screening chat - App-Guided Exercise Therapy for Knee Osteoarthritis (KNEE-MOVE)

Chat with the clinical-trial pre-screening assistant as yourself, about joining the study "App-Guided Exercise Therapy for Knee Osteoarthritis (KNEE-MOVE)".

You are considering signing up and want to know whether you qualify. Let the assistant lead the screening: answer its questions honestly from what you know about yourself, and say so when you do not know a value. Do not volunteer your whole history at once unless that is how you naturally talk.

Continue until the assistant gives you its final eligibility assessment. Once you have received that final assessment, thank the assistant and end the conversation - do not keep chatting after the verdict. This is a preliminary screening conversation, not medical advice - do not invent clinical details you would not know.
