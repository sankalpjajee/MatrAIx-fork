## About this survey

The Health Information National Trends Survey (HINTS) is the National Cancer Institute's nationally representative survey of U.S. adults about how they access and use health information. It measures where people look for health and cancer information and how much they trust it, their internet and technology use (including social media, wearables, telehealth, and patient portals), attitudes and beliefs about cancer and cancer screening, and a range of health behaviors — alongside demographics — to inform health communication and public-health policy.
