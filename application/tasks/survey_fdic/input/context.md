## About this survey

The FDIC National Survey of Unbanked and Underbanked Households is a biennial survey (fielded as a supplement to the Census Bureau's Current Population Survey) that measures U.S. households' access to and use of banking and financial services — bank account ownership, prepaid cards, nonbank financial services, and credit.
