# ClearQueue — concept brief

ClearQueue is a simple app for personal to-dos and appointments. The team is checking pricing before launch.

## Tiers

| Tier | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| Free | $0 | Basic lists, ads, one device |
| Plus | $4.99/mo | No ads, reminders, calendar sync, three devices |
| Pro | $9.99/mo | Everything in Plus, family sharing (up to five people), priority support |

- 7-day trial on Plus or Pro, cancel anytime  
- Annual billing saves about 35% vs monthly  
- Occasional promos (e.g. first month at $1 on Plus)

The product is meant for everyday scheduling — not tied to a specific age group or profession.
